Ways to..

CONTRIBUTE

Donations:

Vipps Community Science Museum #23456. Here you can choose one-time or monthly donations.

Send us a message if you have items or collections you what to donate or lend us, and we’ll get back to you to plan it all out.

Volunteering:

As a volunteer, you can prepare exhibitions, help visitors, and partake in many staff-only activities.

Our volunteers are a valuable part of the group and are encouraged to come with opinions and ideas!

WORK WITH US

Internship

Do you enjoy the fun and excitement of sharing the wonders of science with people? Then you could be the right person for an internship at the museum.

You’ll be learning from several different academics and people passionate about science and sharing it with the broader community.

Research

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to contribute towards them.

Our online records and laboratory space are top of the line. We also collaborate with several universities around the country.

Visit Us

ADMISSION

The entrance is free for all! Guided tours are 70NOK per person. Meet up in reception to join. Just missed one, don’t worry they leave every whole hour. Send us a message for groups of 6 people or more.

Contact us for guided tours of 6 people or more people>

ACCESSIBILITY

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired. Other needs or questions? send us a message

FOOD AND DRINK

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

SHOP

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.

Ways to

Get Involved with us & our affiliates:

Support

There are various ways you can support the museum. Donations are very welcome and are an important way we keep this museum open and accessible to the whole community.

If you should have some item or collection that you think others would enjoy, we would love to display it here at the Museum!

Volunteer

As a voleenter, you assist in a number of exhibithions. You get to see behind the scenes and can partake in, staff-only, lectures.

Once a month you'll join the strategie-lunch, where we all (voleenters involved) discuss ideas for future exhibits and strategies for the museum.

Internship

Do you enjoy the fun and excitement of sharing the wonders of science with people? Then you could be the right person an internship at the museum.

You’ll be learning from a number of different academics and people who are passionate about science and sharing it with the wider community.

Research

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to contribute towards them. Our online records and laboratory space are top of the line. We also collaborate with several universities around the country.

Visit Us

ADMISSION

The entrance is free for all! Guided tours are 70NOK per person. Meet up in reception to join. Just missed one, don’t worry they leave every whole hour. Send us a message for groups of 6 people or more.

Contact us for guided tours of 6 people or more people>

ACCESSIBILITY

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired. Other needs or questions? send us a message

FOOD AND DRINK

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

SHOP

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
